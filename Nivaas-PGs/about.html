About Find Your PG

Find Your PG was founded in 2023 with a simple mission: to make finding the perfect PG accommodation easier, safer, and more transparent for everyone. Our platform connects PG owners with tenants, helping people find their ideal home away from home.

Meet Our Team

Simran Patil

Founder & CEO

With over 10 years of experience in real estate and technology, Simran founded Find Your PG after experiencing her own struggles finding accommodations during her university years. She is passionate about creating living spaces that feel like home.

Priyansh Gupta

CTO

Priyansh leads our technology efforts, ensuring that our platform is secure, reliable, and easy to use. With a background in computer science and data analytics, he has developed smart algorithms that help match tenants with their ideal PG based on preferences and requirements.

Haarshita Mahapatra

Chief of Operations

Haarshita ensures smooth operations between PG owners and tenants. Her background in hospitality and customer service helps her understand the needs of both sides. She personally vets each PG before it's listed on our platform, ensuring quality standards are maintained.

Our Mission

We believe that finding a good place to live shouldn't be stressful or time-consuming. Our platform aims to:

  • Provide transparent and accurate information about PG accommodations
  • Connect tenants with verified and quality PG owners
  • Streamline the PG hunting process with smart filters and search options
  • Ensure safety and security for all users of our platform
  • Create a community of responsible tenants and owners
